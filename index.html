Nuntius

Open source emailing

SASS tools for mass email are expensive, and do not let you full control on your subscribers' data.

Nuntius is an open source software to manage your subscribers, create and send your email campaigns. It can be used as standalone or as a Django app to connect to your existing database. It is currently used in production with hundreds of thousands of subscribers.

Nuntius is shipped with the best open source emailing tools :

  • works well with most ESP thanks to Anymail
  • with Mosaico, design emails that works on every device
