Nuntius

Open source emailing

SASS tools for mass email are expensive, and do not put you in control of your subscribers' data.

Nuntius is an open source software to manage subscribers, and create and send email campaigns. It can be used as a standalone app, or as a Django app to connect to your existing database. It is used in production with hundreds of thousands of subscribers.

Nuntius is shipped with the best open source emailing tools :

  • works well with most ESP thanks to Anymail
  • with Mosaico, design emails that works on every device
